I was left with no AC adapter for my notebook for a few hours on Tuesday.

Having no battery monitor installed and being unable to install one, I was clueless about how much battery life I still had. So I did the best I could at the time: shut down the notebook hacked together a very simple battery monitor by parsing the contents of /proc/acpi/battery/BAT0/state and /proc/acpi/battery/BAT0/info.
